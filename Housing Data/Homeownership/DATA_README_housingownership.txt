File(s): 

	20xx_County_Data_HousingOwnership.csv, 20xx_Tract_Data_HousingOwnership



Used Column(s): 

	Geographic Area Name, id_Fix, percent_Rent, percent_Own, Rent_MOE, Own_MOE


Maps to make:
	Share of population that owns homes (homeownership rate) 
	Share of population that rents homes (rental rate)
	

Column Description:

	id_Fix: 
		Usable part of GEO_ID for leaflet maps

	Geographic Area Name: 
		County, Tract, or State name (ie Malheur County, `Census Tract 201, Canyon County, Idaho`, etc.)

	
	percent_Rent:
		Percent of homes in the tract rented. 
	
	Rent_MOE:
		Percent rent margin of error. 

	percent_Own:
		Percent of homes in the tract owned.

	Own_MOE:
		Percent own margin of error.
